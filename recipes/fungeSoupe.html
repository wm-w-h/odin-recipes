Funges Soup

The Forme of Cury, c. 1390

Take Funges and pare hem clere and dyce hem. take leke and shred him smal and do him to seeþ in gode broth color yt wȝt safron and do þer inne pouder fort and serve hit forth.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 leek, finely sliced
  • 1 tsp. Powder Fort
  • 1 pinch saffron

DIRECTIONS

  1. Combine vegetable broth and saffron in a pot and bring to a simmer.
  2. Add mushrooms and leeks to broth, cook until tender.
  3. Stir in powder fort before serving.
